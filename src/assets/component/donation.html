YEMEN.

Over 6 million people suffer.

About 22 million people in Yemen are in desperate need of help. War and poverty have created the world's worst humanitarian catastrophe where many are at risk of starvation, they are in desperate need of your support.

SYRIA.

6.4 million refugees. 6 million relocated.

More than half a million people have lost their lives in the midst of Syria's civil war. For those who have survived anxiety, hunger, disease and violence, suffering continues. Your donation is a lifeline.

GAZA.

Just over 2 million people under siege for 11 years.

Gaza is under attack and in conflict. The ongoing conflict has left two million people living in poverty. The people of Gaza need your help now. Gaza needs your help now!

SOMALIA.

1.9 million people in need of nutritious food and clean water.

During the summer of 2019, around 2 million people in Somalia were affected by an acute shortage of food, of which 1 million people were acutely malnourished and 175,000 people were severely malnourished. Floods hit the countryside at regular intervals, most recently in the spring of 2020.

ROHINGYA - (Myanmar-Bangladesh).

More than 700,000 Rohingya have fled Myanmar to Bangladesh.

In 2017, Myanmar's military operations against Rakhine State began, forcing over 700,000 Rohingya to flee their villages and from Myanmar to Bangladesh. Today, the Rohingya refugees in Cox's Bazaar in Bangladesh are in very difficult living conditions. Help the Rohingya survive the difficult crisis.
